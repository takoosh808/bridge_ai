The expectation of this hackathon was to create a system that can analyze and derive what creates the best use of the new ai platform the host company had launched.
I created a system that can summarize and derive insight on commits to repositories that companies are working on, and translate them to plain english,
which will allow for less technical users to derive meaning from the updates to projects without having the exact technical logic necessary. For this project, I decided to lean
heavily into leveraging AI to create a product quickly and efficiently in the allotted time, as well as to iterate and test faster to ensure that the product I make works well. 
I also decided to test my product with other users in order to make sure any edge cases I could not think of were covered as well. I participated alone, as I had an idea,
as well as a plan that I knew I could execute quickly. I am still currently participating in this event, as submissions don't end for another week. I have found that most of the
time I spent in this project so far was discovering how the new ai works, and next time I should familiarize myself with more tools before going into the event.
